Paneer Tikka

    Ingredients

    For the Marinade:

  • 250 grams of paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

    • For Skewering and Grilling:

  • Skewers (wooden skewers soaked in water for 30 minutes or metal skewers)
  • Cooking oil for brushing

Instruction:

  1. Prepare Marinade:
    Combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, salt, lemon juice, and oil in a bowl to make a smooth marinade.
  2. Marinate Paneer:
    Coat paneer cubes in the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  3. Skewer Paneer:
    Preheat grill or oven to 200°C (392°F). Thread marinated paneer onto skewers, leaving space between each piece.
  4. Grill Paneer:
    Brush skewered paneer with oil. Grill for 10-15 mins, turning until charred with grill marks.
  5. Serve:
    Slide paneer tikka off skewers onto a plate. Garnish with onions and lemon.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

    Ingredients

    For the Pasta:

  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) of your favorite pasta (e.g., fettuccine, linguine, or spaghetti)
  • Salt for pasta water

    • For the Creamy Garlic Parmesan Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Cook Pasta:
    Boil salted water, add pasta, cook till al dente (8-10 mins), then drain, reserving 1/2 cup water.
  2. Make Sauce:
    Sauté minced garlic in melted butter. Add cream, simmer for 2-3 mins.
  3. Add Cheese:
    Whisk in grated Parmesan until melted and sauce thickens (2-3 mins).
  4. Season and Mix:
    Season with salt and pepper. Adjust consistency with reserved pasta water if too thick.
  5. Combine:
    Toss cooked pasta in the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce until coated.
  6. Serve:
    Transfer to serving plates. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil . Serve hot, and enjoy your delicious pasta!
